Andrew Duncan, #1 RE/MAX Agent in Florida, together with Richard Vasquez of Artisan Insurance, talk about Homeowners and Flood insurance updates. This is the time of the year when people shop around for insurance providers and plan for the next year.
 
They talk about the recent changes in the insurance policies. A lot more companies have come in to the market. Because of the competition, it will be easy to find the best policy for the lowest price.

Richard also advises homeowners to be more proactive because a typical insurance company usually has between two to five thousand customers so it would be hard for them to keep an eye on your policy unless you reach out and ask them.

They also remind homeowners that since it's not hurricane season, there are more companies that are open to new customers looking for flood and hurricane insurance.
